Teaching

In my career, I've provided student-centered semester-long university-level instruction, as well as shorter format research workshops and sessions. On this page, you will find information about this central aspect of my work.

Philosophy

My teaching philosophy and approach to instruction is focused on holistic, experiential education within an inclusive classroom.

Experience

Since 2015, I have taught credit-bearing semester-long courses. In my work as a librarian, I also teach short format lectures and workshops on a variety of scholarly research topics in STEM and science communication.
